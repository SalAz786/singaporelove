03 — MUSIC & NIGHTLIFE

Move with
the city

Singapore's nightlife is rich, diverse and unapologetically alive. From rooftop clubs to Cuban heat, underground selectors to jazz by the river.

CLUB NIGHT
Zouk Singapore — Late Night Sessions

Singapore's legendary nightclub, one of the world's best since 1991. World-class DJs, Phuture's underground room, Capital through house, techno and drum & bass.

Where: 3C River Valley Road  ·  When: Fri & Sat from 22:00  ·  Entry: SGD 25–40 (~€17–€28) incl. 1 drink

CUBAN NIGHT
Havana Nights — CÉ LA VI

Sky-high Cuban night atop Marina Bay Sands. Salsa, rumba and Afro-Cuban beats with live percussion, mojito towers and a panoramic skyline at your feet.

Where: MBS SkyPark Tower 3  ·  When: Thu–Sat from 21:00  ·  Entry: SGD 30–50 (~€20–€34) incl. 1 drink

JAZZ & SOUL
Ronin — Groove Sessions

Intimate jazz bar on Amoy Street. Live jazz, soul and funk, craft cocktails and leather banquettes. Arrive early — it fills fast.

Where: 20 Amoy Street  ·  When: Nightly from 20:00  ·  Entry: Free (cocktails from SGD 18 / ~€12)

ROOFTOP CLUB
1-Altitude — Skyline Saturdays

At 282m, Singapore's highest alfresco bar. Deep house and disco under open skies with 360° views. Smart-casual door policy.

Where: 1 Raffles Place  ·  When: Sat from 21:00  ·  Entry: SGD 35–55 (~€24–€38) incl. 1 drink

BEACH CLUB
Tanjong Beach Club — Sunset Sessions

Sentosa's coolest beach club. Sunday guest DJs, cocktails in the sand, pool and a crowd that knows how to have a good time.

Where: 120 Tanjong Beach Walk, Sentosa  ·  When: Sun from 15:00  ·  Entry: Free (min spend SGD 30 / ~€21)

LIVE MUSIC
Timbre+ — Outdoor Live Stage

Alfresco live music food park at Gillman Barracks. Local and regional bands cover indie to rock while you eat hawker dishes.

Where: 1 Lock Road, Gillman Barracks  ·  When: Nightly from 18:00  ·  Entry: Free

🎵 SINGAPORE ON WAX — VINYL BARS & LISTENING ROOMS

Singapore's vinyl scene is having its moment. From intimate listening rooms with couches and candlelight to design-forward bars where every crackle tells a story — these are the five you need to visit.

DESIGN BAR · TANJONG PAGAR
Offtrack

Two floors of bold design — orange mushroom ceiling lamps, wall-to-wall record art and a serious hi-fi system. One of Singapore's most visually striking bars, with a vinyl selection to match. Order a Negroni and let someone else do the digging.

📍 Tanjong Pagar Road🕗 From 18:00 daily
WINE & VINYL LISTENING ROOM · DUXTON
Appetite

The most intimate on the list. A cosy sofa-filled listening room with floor-to-ceiling vinyl shelves, a temperature-controlled wine fridge and original art on the walls. Buy a listening ticket, pour a glass, and lose yourself in sound. No phones, no rush.

📍 Duxton Road🕗 Evenings only · Book ahead
RECORD SHOP BAR · KEONG SAIK
RPM by D.Bespoke

A serious crate-digger's paradise combined with a proper cocktail bar. Floor-to-ceiling shelves packed with Japanese pressings, soul, funk and jazz rarities. Come to browse, stay for the drinks. The kind of place you'll still be talking about a week later.

📍 Keong Saik Road🕗 Tue–Sun from 15:00
VINYL CAFÉ · LISTENING TICKETS · TANJONG PAGAR
Slow Boat

"Slow rhythms, warm coffee." Buy a 1-hour listening ticket (includes a drink), sit back and let the turntable do the talking. Fresh pastries, specialty coffee and a relaxed come-as-you-are attitude. The perfect morning antidote to a late Zouk night.

📍 Tanjong Pagar🕗 Daily from 09:00 · Listening from SGD 30
RECORDS + PIZZA + CAPS · BRAS BASAH
Choice Cuts Pizza & Records

Only in Singapore: a record shop that also sells excellent pizza and snapback caps. Brilliant funk, soul and hip-hop selection, a loyal local following and the best casual lunch spot near the National Museum. Grab a slice, flip through the crates.

06 — ESSENTIALS

Good to know

Visa & Entry

Italian passport: visa-free 30 days. UAE & most nationalities: visa-free or on-arrival. Verify for your specific passport before travel.

Currency

Singapore Dollar (SGD). Cards accepted everywhere. ATMs at every MRT station. ~1 EUR = 1.45 SGD. ~1 GBP = 1.72 SGD.

Weather in May

26–33°C, humid. Afternoon tropical showers possible. Light linen, small umbrella and good sunscreen essential.

Getting Around

MRT is excellent, cheap at SGD 1–3/trip. Grab for late nights ~SGD 10–25. Walking easy around Marina Bay.

Airport Transfer

Changi to city: ~25 min taxi (SGD 25–40). To Mandai: ~30 min taxi (SGD 35–45). MRT from T2/T3 to city: SGD 2.50.
